About the Pick Me Test

How the Pick Me Test Works

The Pick Me Test simulates a social media feed with 10 realistic SNS posts. For each post, you choose how to react: leave a comment, hit like, or scroll past. Your reactions are analyzed to determine your pick-me energy level, revealing patterns in how you engage with social media content.

Understanding Pick-Me Energy

Pick-me energy refers to behaviors aimed at seeking validation or standing out on social media. This test measures these tendencies in a fun, judgment-free way. Whether you score high or low, it is all about self-awareness and having a laugh with friends.
